# Content Operations and Analytics Map

## Event Tracking (privacy-safe)
- `catalog_search`: when product search form submits.
- `compare_toggle`: when a product is added/removed from compare selection.
- `filter_change`: when category filters change (URL `filter` param updates).
- `inquiry_click`: when inquiry CTA is clicked (recommended via tag manager click rule).

## Refresh Cadence
- Hero/banner copy: every 4 weeks.
- Partnership logos: monthly review; refresh quarterly.
- Testimonial/review placeholders: replace with approved quotes every release.
- Featured projects block: rotate every 2 weeks.
- FAQ content: review monthly or after each delivery sprint.

## QA Checks
- Confirm tracked events only fire after consent rules are satisfied.
- Validate all updated content paths on mobile/tablet/desktop.
